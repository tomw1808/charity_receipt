Known Problems:

  1. The Star WebPRNT App doesn't have access to a console. That means debugging is a pain. Whatever I tried, it will loose the connection every 10-15 minutes to the Infura Websocket Server. So, instead of very complicated tries to reconnect, I opted for a page-refresh every 10 minutes. This takes around 500ms - if your donation is sent at exactly this time it's not printed. But thanks for it anyways.
  2. I tried to use Web3 Beta-38, which might fixes this issue, but get weird errors in combination with WebPack. Now, I'm not a webpack pro, so I went back to Web3 Beta-37
  3. I will try to keep things running, but don't blame me if they go down. It should be usable in production, but you never know...
